About Us

{{ site.org_full_description }}

Dr. David Ifeoluwa Adelani

Director

Dr. Adelani is an Assistant Professor at McGill, Core Academic Member at Mila and Canada CIFAR AI Chair. He works on NLP for African languages.

Dr. Ezekiel Soremekun

Director

Dr. Soremekun is an Assistant Professor at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), Singapore. He leads the Trustworthy SoftWare & AI (TrustWare) research group at SUTD.

Jesujoba O. Alabi

Director

Jesujoba is a PhD student in Computer Science at Saarland University, Germany. He works on NLP for low-resource languages, speech processing, and interpretability of NLP models.

Research Projects

We have contributed and led research projects such as

NiLE

Empowering Education Through Language Technology.

MasakhaNER

Named Entity Recognition for African Languages.

MAFAND-MT

Machine Translation for African Languages.

WorldCuisines

Multilingual and Multicultural Visual Question Answering.

Masakhane-NLU

Conversation AI and Benchmark datasets for African languages.

BibleTTS

A large, high-fidelity, multilingual, and uniquely African speech corpus.

AfriQA

Cross-lingual question answering (QA) dataset for African languages.

Afri-MCQA

Multimodal Cultural Question Answering for African Languages.
